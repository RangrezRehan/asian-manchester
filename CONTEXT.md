## Business Concept (Phase 1 – Step 1)

Asian Manchester provides authentic Indian and Pakistani catering for small events of 20–40 guests across Manchester and surrounding areas. The focus is on freshly prepared curries, rice dishes, breads, and traditional desserts, delivered for:

- Private gatherings
- Birthdays
- Family celebrations
- Community events

Key goals:

- Bring rich South Asian flavours to small events.
- Use high-quality ingredients and traditional recipes.
- Offer reliable and consistent catering service.
- Offer clear pricing based on number of guests and menu selection.

## Menu Structure (High-Level)

- **Mains**: Curries and gravies (e.g., Nihari, Butter Chicken, Chicken Tikka Masala, Chicken Karahi, Lamb Karahi, Dal, Mixed Vegetable, Manchurian).
- **Rice dishes**: Typically the base of packages (e.g., Chicken Biryani, Kabuli Pulao, Lamb Leg Roast with Rose Bukhari Rice).
- **Sides**: Breads and rice accompaniments (e.g., Naan, Roti, Boiled Rice, Pilau Rice, Fried Rice).
- **Starters**: Light dishes served before mains (e.g., Pepper Winglets, Chaat, Paneer Tikka).
- **Soups**: Optional seasonal add-ons (e.g., Hot & Sour, Lentil, Manchow).
- **Desserts / sweets**: Traditional desserts, often with ice cream (e.g., Gajrela, Gulab Jamun, Ras Malai, Matanjan, Suji Shahi Halwa).

The website should later make it easy for visitors to:

- Understand what categories exist.
- See example dishes in each category.
- Identify and compare packages.

## Pricing Packages (Concept)

- **Basic Package**  
  - Chicken Biryani or Kabuli Pulao  
  - 1 curry  
  - Raita & salad  
  - Dessert  
  - Target price: £12 per person  

- **Standard Package**  
  - Rice dish  
  - 2 curries  
  - Naan  
  - Raita & salad  
  - Dessert  
  - Target price: £15 per person  

- **Premium Package**  
  - Premium rice dish  
  - 2 premium curries  
  - Starter  
  - Naan  
  - Dessert  
  - Target price: £20 per person  

These prices and inclusions are conceptual for the website content and can be refined later.

## Core vs Premium vs Add-Ons

- **Core dishes** (popular, work well in bulk, travel well, stable ingredient cost):  
  - Mains: Butter Chicken, Chicken Karahi, Chicken Tikka Masala, Dal, Mixed Vegetable  
  - Rice: Chicken Biryani, Kabuli Pulao  
  - Desserts: Gulab Jamun, Ras Malai  

- **Premium dishes** (higher cost or longer cooking time):  
  - Nihari  
  - Lamb Karahi  
  - Lamb Leg Roast with Rose Bukhari Rice  
  - Matanjan  
  - Gajrela  

- **Optional / add-ons** (extras, not usually in base packages):  
  - Starters: Pepper Winglets, Chaat, Paneer Tikka  
  - Soups: Hot & Sour Soup, Lentil Soup, Manchow Soup  

The website should present these as clearly separated: core options in standard packages, premium upgrades, and optional add-ons.
