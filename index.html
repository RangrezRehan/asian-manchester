Our Story

Asian Manchester is a catering-only business. No restaurant, no takeaway—just dedicated delivery catering for your special occasions.

We specialise in authentic Indian and Pakistani cuisine for small events of 10–40 guests across Manchester. Family birthdays, private parties, community events, cultural celebrations and corporate lunches—we bring the flavours to you.

Our Approach
